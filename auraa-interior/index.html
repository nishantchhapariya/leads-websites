Meet the Designer

Design is the Silent Ambassador of Your Brand

"Your home should tell the story of who you are, and be a collection of what you love."

I am Shubhra Sharma, the founder of Auraa Interior. Based out of our design studio at Virendra Vilas in City Center, Gwalior, I focus on creating warm, organic modern interiors. We balance rich wooden textures, soft warm linens, natural stone, and gold accents to form spaces that feel both luxurious and welcoming.

Whether it is a full villa remodel, a modular kitchen layout, or an office space design, our process is highly personalized. We listen closely to your lifestyle requirements and craft a tailored visual language that is completely unique to your home.
