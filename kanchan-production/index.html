About Our Studio

Three Decades of
Visual Artistry

Every wedding is an unrepeatable masterpiece. Led by Manoj Verma, our mission is to capture raw emotions, fleeting smiles, and royal celebrations so they remain preserved for generations.

Located on Tansen Road in Hazira, Gwalior, Kanchan Production is a professional collective of wedding photographers, cinematographers, and editors. Combining classical portrait techniques with contemporary storytelling, we turn your big day into an heirloom film.
